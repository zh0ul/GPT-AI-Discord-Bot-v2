> # Character Card Importer
> ## Import your first card with the following command:
> `${p}import ${url}`
> ## Next, download more character cards and import them.
> 1. **Download** a Character Card
> 2. **Drag and drop** the image into this chat (or press upload file)
> 3. **Press Enter (or send)**
> ## You can get Character Cards from:
> - [**Pygmalion - SFW**](<https://pygmalion.chat/characters>)
//> - [**Character Hub - NSFW**](<https://www.chub.ai/>)
> ## You can **create** and **edit** cards **online** using:
> - [**ZoltanAI**](<https://zoltanai.github.io/character-editor/>)
> ## You can **create**, **edit** and **test** cards locally using:
> - [**Oobabooga**](<https://github.com/oobabooga/text-generation-webui>)
> - [**Silly Tavern**](<https://github.com/SillyTavern/SillyTavern>)
